What is RunMap?

RunMap is an app runners, bikers and hikers can use to find their next favorite route to do activities in.

How do I submit a route?

Submitting a route is easy! Go to the "Submit a Route" tab below and enter details for your route. Click Next and you'll be asked to draw the route on a map. Click "Submit" and you're done!

How do I view submitted routes?

To view submitted routes, go to the "Find a Route" tab below and enter details for the type of route you want to find!
